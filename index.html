About Me

I am a focused and reliable professional with over nineteen years of hands on experience in analyzing complex data to meet business demands. I regularly use strong verbal and written communication to effectively disseminate information to internal and external trading partners.

My background has been in healthcare data, with a focus primarily on Medicaid. I enjoy working on complicated projects using a variety of technologies. I am a problem solver that utilizes unique and thoughtful process solutions.

My free time is spent with my family, motorcycling and tinkering with old cars.
